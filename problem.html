Problem Statement

For our program we are going to develop a game in which players can have battles. The goal is for a simple turn based battle system to be implemented, in which a player may fight npc enemies. The player will have a set of moves which they may use to win the combat. Player characters will have stats which help determine their performance. There will be types which have specialities, and counters.


Requirements

Necessary

Combat system, based on turns where each turn players may use one of several moves available to them.

Player character will have a series of stats, which helps determine how well the character performs. These stats may include health, speed, evasiveness, or accuracy.

Moves may: heal player health, damage opponent health, buff player stats, debuff opponent stats, buff/debuff general types.

Health: primary part of combat, when hits zero, combat is over.

Speed: determines which player/opponent takes the first turn.

Evasiveness: determines likelihood of character dodging opponent’s attack. (counters accuracy)

Accuracy: determines likelihood of attacks landing. (counters evasiveness)

Types are a set of elements which the player character may specialize in, each will have various advantages/disadvantages with regard to other types, and will have their own specialities. Likely 3 - 4 types total.

AI opponents (so that player may have something to fight).

Default characters: If we don’t get to leveling systems or customizability, we may have characters that the player may select (likely one per type). These characters would have a set appearance and move set.

Desired

Leveling system in which winning combat leads to experience earned. Level up may offer stat increase, or new move availability.

Currency dropped by opponents so that the player may purchase items.

Items: things that the player may equip to buff themselves, or change looks.

Stat: Attack: determines overall damage output.

Stat: Defense: counteracts the attack stat.

Optional

Looks: player customizable character.

Online play: The ability for the player to connect to and battle other players.

Hub: Area where stores/online chat may take place. (without a hub we may have a menu screen which facilitates store actions / online play.
